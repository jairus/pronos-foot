Rules

The goal is to forecast the winner and score of each game of the competition. The score taken into account is the one after the official time (extra time included).

Here are some examples on the football game France-Switzerland :    

  • You think that France will win the match after 90 minutes: you should forecast 3-0 for France
  • ou think that the two teams will be at 1-1 after 90 minutes but that France will win 3-1 after the extra time: you should forecast 3-1 for France
  • You think that the two teams will be at 1-1 after 90 minutes, 2-2 after 120 minutes and that France will win the shootout by 5 penalties to 4:      you should forecast 2-2

The number of points you earn for your forecasts depend on:

  • The round of the competition the game belongs to (group matches, round of 16...)
  • The quality of your forecast:
  • winner match only
  • winner and score match
